About Me

I am a dedicated educator and Tamil language specialist with a passion for preserving and promoting Tamil literature and culture. Currently teaching at Puteri Wangsa Secondary School, I hold a Bachelor’s in Education (Tamil Language) with First Class Honours and am pursuing a Master’s in Education at Sultan Idris Educational University.

My professional journey includes roles as a news presenter, literary project assistant, and tutor, complemented by leadership in academic and cultural societies. I actively contribute to Tamil linguistics through conference presentations, journal publications, and literary reviews.
