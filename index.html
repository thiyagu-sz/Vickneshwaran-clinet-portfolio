A dedicated educator and Tamil language specialist with a strong commitment to Tamil literature and culture. Vickneshwaran has a multifaceted professional background, including roles as a news presenter, literary project assistant, and tutor. He is actively involved in academic and cultural societies, contributing to Tamil linguistics through conference presentations, journal publications, and literary reviews. Currently pursuing a Master’s in Education at Sultan Idris Educational University. Held significant positions in academic and cultural societies, showcasing leadership in fostering Tamil language and culture.
